Overview

Code Kages is an Android-based Wealth Management System developed over multiple iterations, focusing on helping users track and manage their financial activities efficiently. The project evolved through careful planning and iterative development.

Iterative Development

Developed through four iterations with continuous improvement

Team Collaboration

Effective teamwork with regular meetings and clear communication

Project Management

Organized development with detailed documentation and tracking

Vision

Our vision is to create a comprehensive wealth management solution that helps users track their financial activities, analyze spending patterns, and make informed financial decisions while maintaining a clean and intuitive user interface.

"Building a wealth management system that combines simplicity with powerful financial tracking features, making financial management accessible to everyone."

  • Value of regular team communication

  • Benefits of iterative development
